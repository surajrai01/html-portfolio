Om-Dar-B-Dar

The whole film is like a journey through dreams. Excellent cinematography and the credit goes to the indigenous treatment
which is rare & innovative. The background score has been used differently, sometimes it's chaos but it brings a
hallucinatory effect, however dramatic and pleasant. In true sense its a cult film.
